## 🚀 1321. Restaurant Growth

### 💡 Idea

The goal is to calculate the **7-day moving average** of the restaurant's revenue.

For each day (starting from the 7th day), we need to report:

- The current date.
- The total revenue over the **current day and the previous 6 days**.
- The average daily revenue over those 7 days.

We first calculate the total revenue for each day, then use window functions to compute the rolling 7-day sum and average.

---

## 💻 Solution

```sql
SELECT visited_on, amount, ROUND(amount / 7, 2) AS average_amount
FROM (
    SELECT visited_on,
    SUM(amount) OVER (ORDER BY visited_on ROWS BETWEEN 6 PRECEDING AND CURRENT ROW) AS amount,
    ROW_NUMBER() OVER (ORDER BY visited_on) AS rn
    FROM (
        SELECT visited_on,
        SUM(amount) AS amount
        FROM Customer
        GROUP BY visited_on
    ) t
) s
WHERE rn >= 7;
```
---

### 🛠️ How It Works

1. Group the `Customer` table by `visited_on` to calculate the total revenue for each day.
2. Use `SUM() OVER` with a window frame to calculate the rolling 7-day revenue.
3. Use `ROW_NUMBER()` to number the days in chronological order.
4. Exclude the first six days since a complete 7-day window is not available.
5. Divide the 7-day total by `7` to obtain the average daily revenue.
6. Round the average to **2 decimal places**.

---

### ⏱️ Complexity

**Time Complexity:** `O(n log n)` *(sorting required for window functions)*

**Space Complexity:** `O(n)` *(stores daily totals and running window values)*
