## 🚀 1070. Product Sales Analysis III

### 💡 Idea

The goal is to find the **first year** each product was sold, along with its **quantity** and **price** in that year.

We first determine the earliest (`MIN`) sales year for each product. Then, we join this result back with the `Sales` table to retrieve the corresponding `quantity` and `price`.

---

### 💻 Solution

```sql
SELECT s.product_id, s.first_year, t.quantity, t.price
FROM (
    SELECT product_id, MIN(year) AS first_year
    FROM Sales
    GROUP BY product_id
) AS s
INNER JOIN Sales t
ON s.product_id = t.product_id
AND s.first_year = t.year;
```

---

### 🛠️ How It Works

1. Group the `Sales` table by `product_id`.
2. Find the earliest (`MIN`) sales year for each product.
3. Join the result back with the `Sales` table.
4. Match both the `product_id` and the first sales year.
5. Return the product ID, first year, quantity, and price.

---

### ⏱️ Complexity

**Time Complexity:** `O(n)` *(aggregation followed by a join)*

**Space Complexity:** `O(n)` *(stores the first year for each product)*
