## 🚀 1050. Actors and Directors Who Cooperated At Least Three Times

### 💡 Idea

The goal is to find all **actor-director pairs** that have worked together **at least three times**.

Since each row in the `ActorDirector` table represents one collaboration, we can group the records by both `actor_id` and `director_id`, then keep only those pairs whose collaboration count is **3 or more**.

---

### 💻 Solution

```sql
SELECT actor_id, director_id
FROM ActorDirector
GROUP BY actor_id, director_id
HAVING COUNT(*) >= 3;
```

---

### 🛠️ How It Works

1. Group the records by both `actor_id` and `director_id`.
2. Count the number of collaborations for each actor-director pair.
3. Use the `HAVING` clause to keep only pairs with **3 or more** collaborations.
4. Return the corresponding `actor_id` and `director_id`.

---

### ⏱️ Complexity

**Time Complexity:** `O(n)` *(single scan with grouping)*

**Space Complexity:** `O(n)` *(stores grouped actor-director pairs)*
