## 🚀 1757. Recyclable and Low Fat Products

### 💡 Idea

The goal is to find the IDs of products that are:

- **Low fat** (`low_fats = 'Y'`)
- **Recyclable** (`recyclable = 'Y'`)

We simply filter the `Products` table using both conditions.

---

## 💻 Solution

```sql
SELECT product_id
FROM Products
WHERE low_fats = 'Y' AND recyclable = 'Y';
```

---

### 🛠️ How It Works

1. Read the records from the `Products` table.
2. Filter products where:
   - `low_fats = 'Y'`
   - `recyclable = 'Y'`
3. Return the corresponding `product_id`.

---

### ⏱️ Complexity

**Time Complexity:** `O(n)` *(single scan of the table)*

**Space Complexity:** `O(1)`
